Scoring Rubric

These formulas match the server-side grader and help judges map precision, recall, reward, speed, and collateral damage into the final normalized score.
task_spam
easy
Score = 0.7 × F1 + 0.3 × sigmoid(mean_reward / 50). High F1 dominates, but reward still matters.
task_misinfo
medium
Score = 0.6 × F1 + 0.4 × max(0, 1 − mean_hop / 20). Early intervention is rewarded explicitly.
task_cib
hard
Score = 0.5 × recall + 0.5 × F1 − min(collateral_rate × 2, 0.5). Removing bots is good, harming real users is very costly.
